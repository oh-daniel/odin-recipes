Fried Chicken

Description

A fun and easy fried chicken recipe to make with your family. Crispy, juicy, and tender fried chicken.

Ingredients

  • 30 saltine crackers
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons dry potato flakes
  • 1 teaspoon seasoned salt
  • 0.5 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 egg
  • 8 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups vegetable oil

Directions

  1. Place crackers in a large resealable plastic bag; seal bag and crush crackers until coarse. Add flour, potato flakes, seasoned salt, and pepper and mix well
  2. Beat egg in shallow bowl. One by one, dredge chicken pieces in egg, then place in bag with crumb mixture. Seal bag and shake to coat
  3. Heat oil in deep-fryer or saucepan to 350 degrees F
  4. Fry chicken, turning frequently until golden brown and juices run clear; 15 to 20 minutes.
