Chicken Caesar Salad

Description

A classic recipe for a chicken Caesar salad. Delicious, satisfying, and a perfect combination of romaine, dressing, chicken, and cheese for any day.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups chopped romaine lettuce
  • 1 pound cooked boneless skinless chicken breast, cut into strips
  • 0.5 cup shredded parmesan cheese
  • 0.5 cup seasoned croutons
  • 0.25 cup Caesar dressing

Directions

  1. Cover 4 plates with lettuce
  2. Top with all remaining ingredients except dressing
  3. Drizze with dressing just before serving
